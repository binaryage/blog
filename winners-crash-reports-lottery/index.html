The Crash Reports Lottery - grab your free licenses

I promised some fun here and here is the announcement of the lottery winners.
I have here almost $1000 worth of licenses for lucky crash reporters.

How does the lottery work?

I've had over 11000 crash reports during this year. That is a scary number. But the big portion of them were infamous crashes of the broken expiration code in 0.9.5. That is fair, everyone had a chance to send a crash report at some point although it caused some sleepless nights to me ;-) Maybe you wonder how I managed to process so many reports. They are just emails and I made a smart decision to use gmail as a crash-report database because it has the best search on the planet.

I decided to draw 64 unique winners, so nobody can win two or more licenses. Also I decided to honor the number of crash reports you've sent so every email counts as a new ticket in the lottery.
